Uzhavarbumi Agro Products Pvt Ltd, founded in July 2017, is a fresh dairy products company based in Maduranthakam, Chengalpattu, India. UB's prime motive is to establish direct trade between local farmers and consumers. UB provides products including farm-fresh Milk, Ghee, Honey and Palkova. Today UB is one of Tamilnadu's leading brands, merchandising fresh dairy products.
